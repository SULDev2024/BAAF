08 Maximum Parsimony (MP)

MP follows Occam’s razor: minimize evolutionary steps. Informative sites (≥2 states, each in ≥2 sequences) drive the search; for four taxa all three unrooted quartets are scored with Fitch’s algorithm. With more taxa, exhaustive search explodes—use branch-and-bound or heuristics (NNI, SPR) in PAUP* or IQ-TREE.
